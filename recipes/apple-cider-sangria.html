Apple Cider Sangria

Description

The perfect Thanskgiving coctail that will keep guests coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon wedge
  • 1/4 cup cinnamon-sugar
  • 1 24-oz bottle sparkling cider, chilled
  • 1 bottle sweet white wine, chilled
  • 1/3 cup Fireball Whiskey
  • 2 apples, thinly sliced
  • Cinnamon sticks for serving

Instructions

  1. Rim each glass with a lemon wedge and dip in cinnamon sugar.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine cider, wine, and Fireball. Add sliced apples and stir to combine.
  3. Server into prepared glasses and garnish with an apple slice and cinnamon stick.
